★ About me!! _

HI!!! My name is Ryan Polasky and welcome to my home on the world wide web! I am a junior software engineer who just graduated computer science from the University of Texas at Dallas (May 2026).

I've been writing code since I was 12 and shipping it for Apple since 2024 - two summers in Cupertino on the Camera & Photos team, building LLM-powered developer tools that turn natural-language questions into SQL/NoSQL queries and shrink test triage time by ~98%. ♥

At UTD I led the dev side of ACM, mentored four junior devs through internships, and helped run HackUTD - the largest collegiate hackathon in the country (1,200+ participants!!!).

I also write lo-fi piano in FL Studio (you can find me on Spotify as plasky!), maintain a public CS notes garden, and answer questions for Apple customers part-time as an At-Home Advisor (2× Excellence Award winner!!).

  • Languages: Python · TypeScript · HTML/CSS · Swift · SQL
  • Currently into: agentic systems · LLM observability · hand-rolled diagrammatic UIs
  • Looking for: a junior SWE role starting May 2026!
  • Fun fact: I once shipped 300+ healthcare-system devices in a single intake (department record).
