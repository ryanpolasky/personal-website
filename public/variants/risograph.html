About the writer.

Liner notes · 1,400 words

I'm a Computer Science graduate from UT Dallas (May 2026). I specialise in solving the unglamorous problems that make engineering teams 10× faster — intelligent log analysis, bulletproof CI/CD pipelines, test automation that actually works, and AI-powered query generation that doesn't fall over the moment a user phrases a question oddly.

At Apple, I built LLM-powered tooling for the Camera and Photos team, automated the triage of thousands of test failures with smart categorization, and designed cloud workflows that ship fast without breaking anything. I balance velocity with reliability, security with usability, and prioritize crystal-clear communication.

Off the clock I produce lo-fi music as plasky (published since 2023), maintain a public knowledge base of my CS curriculum, and keep a close eye on the things that go wrong in production at 3am — that's where the lessons live.

Most production fires were started by a TODO that nobody undid.
